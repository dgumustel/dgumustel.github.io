Born and raised in the Pacific Northwest, I've always loved the evergreen Cascades and overcast Pacific beaches. After earning my bachelor's degree in oceanography from the University of Washington in 2020, I earned my certificate in data science through an immersive program from General Assembly in 2021.

More intriguing information

I'm currently building a career for myself at the intersection of oceanography and data science, leading me to join an ocean acoustics team at the University of Washington to conduct multi-year analysis of OOI data and help develop open-access tools to make sonar data more accessible to the public.

So in conclusion ...

I care a lot about the environment, but ultimately the most important thing to me is people - the people I live with, the people I work with, and the communities I give back to. I love to learn, I love to help others learn, and I'm always looking for ways to contribute more to the oceanography and data science communities!
